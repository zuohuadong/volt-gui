# Product Requirements

## Purpose

Help product and engineering teams turn ambiguous requests into implementable, testable work.

## Workflow

1. Identify user, problem, business goal, constraints, and non-goals.
2. Define user flows, edge cases, permissions, and data requirements.
3. Write acceptance criteria and testable outcomes.
4. Split work into milestones and dependencies.
5. Surface risks and open questions.

## Output

Return:

- Problem statement.
- Goals and non-goals.
- User stories.
- Acceptance criteria.
- Milestones.
- Risks and open questions.
