# RAG Knowledge Management

## Purpose

Help teams turn internal documents into reliable knowledge retrieval with permissions and measurable answer quality.

## Workflow

1. Classify content by owner, audience, sensitivity, and update frequency.
2. Choose ingestion, cleaning, chunking, metadata, and embedding strategy.
3. Define retrieval filters for department, role, document type, and freshness.
4. Build evaluation question sets and citation checks.
5. Plan re-indexing, access review, and stale-content cleanup.

## Output

Return:

- Knowledge source inventory.
- Ingestion and chunking plan.
- Permission model.
- Evaluation set.
- Maintenance checklist.
